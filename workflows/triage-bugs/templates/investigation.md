# Investigation ticket template

## What we are seeing

Observed behavior, impact, frequency, and source.

## Evidence so far

What has been verified, including failed reproduction attempts.

## Questions to answer

- What condition triggers the behavior?
- Which component owns the failure?
- Is data, security, or reliability at risk?
- What instrumentation or access is missing?

## Suggested next steps

Concrete queries, logs, tests, or owners to unblock the investigation.
